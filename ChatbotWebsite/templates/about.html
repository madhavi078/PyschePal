About Us

Welcome to PsychePal! This your AI powered Mental Health Companion which is is designed to provide reliable information and resources to help individuals improve their mental health and wellbeing.

We understand that seeking help for mental health concerns can be challenging, and PsychePal is designed to make the process as comfortable and accessible as possible.

However, please remember that PsychePal is not a substitute for professional help. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please seek help from a mental health professional.

Our Mission

We believe that education and awareness are critical components of mental health. PsychePal aimed to provide accurate and up-to-date information on various mental health conditions, including symptoms, causes and effects. We also provide a variety of resouces, such as mental health tests and exercises, to help you evaluate your mental health and improve your overall wellbeing.

Our Team

PsychePal is developed by dedicated college students who are passionate about mental health. The developer is committed to play a part in breaking down the stigma surrounding mental health and create a supportive community for individuals to seek help. Thank you for choosing PsychePal as your first step towards a healthier and happier life! We are here to support you every step of the way.
